# Lexfly Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Lexfly ("we", "our", or "us") provides an English vocabulary learning experience built around reading, video transcripts, saved words, and study activities.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
If you sign in, we may collect basic account information such as your email address and a user identifier.

### User Content
We may store content you add or generate in the app, including:

- saved words
- reading history
- article links or pasted text
- transcript-related study content

### Camera and Photo Library Access
If you choose to use camera or gallery features, Lexfly may process images you provide in order to extract text for learning purposes.

### Audio and Pronunciation
If you use pronunciation playback or related speech features, requests may be sent to services that help return audio playback.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide vocabulary learning features
- save your study progress
- show saved words and reading history
- process text from articles, transcripts, camera captures, or imported images
- improve reliability and core product functionality

## Tracking

Lexfly is not designed to track you across third-party apps and websites for advertising purposes.

## Data Sharing

We may use third-party service providers to support app functionality, such as authentication, storage, text processing, or speech features. We do not sell your personal information.

## Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the app's features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Your Choices

You can choose not to use optional features such as camera, gallery, or account sign-in. You may also contact us to request support regarding your data.

## Children's Privacy

Lexfly is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law without parental or guardian involvement.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
